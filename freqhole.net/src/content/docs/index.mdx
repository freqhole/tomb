## what is freqhole?

a self-hosted music server and player: share your music library with yourself and your friends.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="self-hosted" icon="laptop">
    run on your own hardware (like a raspberry pi); D.I.Y. or D.I.E.
  </Card>
  <Card title="P2P streaming" icon="rocket">
    share with friends without port forwarding or dynamic DNS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="works offline" icon="moon">
    caches music locally, keep listening without internet. sync yr tunez to yr
    mobile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="cross-platform" icon="puzzle">
    desktop app for macOS and Linux. CLI for linux geekz && headless servers.
    web app with offline support for mobile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="your files are read-only" icon="document">
    freqhole reads your music but never writes, moves, renames, or deletes your
    filez! everything is indexed in a sqlite database.
  </Card>
  <Card title="music brainz" icon="magnifier">
    look up album info, track details, and cover art from MusicBrainz and Cover
    Art Archive.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## which client is right for you?

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="on your desktop or laptop" icon="laptop">
    use the **[desktop app (charnel)](/getting-started/desktop-setup/)** — a GUI
    player and server management wizard, or the
    **[CLI](/getting-started/cli-setup/)** if you prefer the terminal or are
    running a headless server.
  </Card>
  <Card title="on your phone" icon="phone">
    use the **[web app](https://spume.freqhole.net)** in your mobile browser —
    it caches your library and music offline, so it keeps working when your
    connection drops.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
